Coming in from out-of-town?

The closest airport to the wedding venue is Victoria International Airport (YYJ). We recommend that guests fly into YYJ if air travel is required to get to the event.

If you do not need to fly into town, but would prefer to travel by road we recommend booking a spot on BC Ferries from the Tsawwassen Ferry Terminal to the Swartz Bay Ferry Terminal.
