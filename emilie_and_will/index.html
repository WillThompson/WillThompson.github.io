Details

WHAT TO WEAR

Suggested dress is semi-formal or garden party (wear what makes you comfortable, please don't wear a tuxedo, please don't wear shorts).

Please be mindful of soft and uneven surfaces when selecting footwear as Sea Cider is a working farm and the ceremony will take place in the adjacent forest.

CELL PHONE USE

We ask that you kindly remember to turn off your phones during the ceremony. We also ask that guests refrain from taking photos during the ceremony as we have hired a professional photographer to document the event. Please feel welcome to take as many photos as you like after the ceremony.

FOOD & DRINK

Please indicate if you have any dietary restrictions when you RSVP. Beer, wine, and cider will be served at the open bar until 10:15pm.

Travel

GETTING HERE

By Air. The closest airport to the wedding venue is Victoria International Airport (YYJ).

By Land and Sea. If you are coming via Vancouver you will need to take a ferry to get to Victoria. Reservations on BC Ferries in summer months are highly recommended if you are planning to bring your car on the ferry. Space fills up quickly so please consider booking in advance. You will be traveling from the Tsawwassen Ferry Terminal (Vancouver) to the Swartz Bay Ferry Terminal (Victoria). If you are coming via Seattle you can take The Clipper foot passanger ferry (advanced tickets are recommended) or The Coho car ferry from Port Angeles.

WHERE TO STAY

We recomend staying in downtown Victoria in the inner harbour area. A range of hotels, motels, and airbnbs are available to fit different price points. Victoria is a popular tourist destination in the summer so consider advance booking.

TRANSPORTATION TO THE VENUE

The wedding venue is 20km north of downtown Victoria. A shuttle will be provided from downtown Victoria to the wedding venue. Please indicate on your RSVP if you would like to take the shuttle (aka. party bus). Overnight parking is also available at the venue. Private car service can be arranged upon request.
